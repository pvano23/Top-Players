1 Jaden McDuffie ▼
Position: WR (X)
Class: Class Of 2026
Team: South River High School (Edgewater, MD, US)
Height: 6’3”
Weight: 160 Grade: D1 FCS level prospect (81.600)

PLAYER SUMMARY: McDuffie is my top overall player in his class and at his position. I firmly believe he can compete at the D1 FCS level and should draw serious interest from multiple programs at that tier. The only factor currently holding him back from FBS-level opportunities is his weight, but that’s an easily correctable aspect of his game. 1 Kellen Wymer ▼
Position: OT
Class: Class Of 2027
Team: Liberty Center High School (Liberty Center, OH, US)
Height: 6’5”
Weight: 265
Grade: D1 FBS level prospect (83.370)

PLAYER SUMMARY: Wymer is currently my top prospect in the class of 2027. He possesses outstanding size and elite athletic traits that immediately stand out on film. While unverified, his reported 4.91 speed is highly impressive—and in my opinion, it translates well on tape. His relentless motor as an offensive lineman is a key differentiator, as he constantly pursues defenders and looks to dominate every rep. His combination of powerful leg drive and upper-body strength makes him a force to be reckoned with. He’s a player every program should have on their radar and one who should start earning offers quickly.

4 David Nosa ▼
Position: RB
Class: Class Of 2027
Team: Paul Dwyer High School (Oshawa, ON, Canada)
Height: 6’1”
Weight: 198
Grade: D1 FCS level prospect (70.037)

PLAYER SUMMARY: Nosa is one of the top prospects in the 2027 class and the best running back in his group. His natural ability, physical tools, and impressive ball carrier moves make him stand out. While he lacks true breakaway speed and projects as a 4.7-second 40-yard dash runner, he’s more quick than fast, which suits his short-yardage style. Many elite backs run in the 4.5s, so his long speed isn’t a major concern. He still has room to grow physically, and playing at American Prep will expose him to stronger competition, helping him elevate his game even further.

5 Xander Odenyo ▼
Position: WR Class: Class Of 2027
Team: St Francis (Calgary, AB, Canada)
Height: 6’2”
Weight: 185
Grade: D1 FCS level prospect (70.0)
PLAYER SUMMARY: Odenyo is a highly talented receiver who deserves consideration at all levels of play. He’s a physical playmaker who can power through defenders while also displaying the agility to make them miss in open space. With his skill set, he could thrive as a bigger slot receiver, as he’s dangerous with the ball in his hands and capable of creating big plays on the ground. Additionally, he excels in contested catch situations, making him a major red-zone threat with multiple ways to impact a defense. His versatility makes him a player who should rarely come off the field, especially given his effectiveness as a run blocker. Despite his already impressive skill set, he’s still young with significant upside. I believe he has the potential to develop into the top wide receiver in Canada for his class.
